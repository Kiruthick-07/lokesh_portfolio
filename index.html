01 — About

Building the future
one system at a time

I'm Lokesh S T, a third-year B.E. Mechatronics Engineering student at Kongu Engineering College, Perundurai, with a strong CGPA of 8.76. My work sits at the intersection of mechanical systems, electronics, and software.

I've been recognized by IIT Madras for my innovation in sustainable agricultural engineering — developing a non-electrical solar tracker that earned a ₹50,000 stipend. I believe the best engineering is invisible: systems that just work, beautifully.

My interests span electron devices and digital circuits, manufacturing processes, and the rapidly evolving world of Internet of Things. I'm actively involved in KEC's Robotic Society and ISTE, pushing the boundaries of what student engineers can build.
